Top 10 Tourist Spots in the Philippines

#1 El Nido, Palawan

Famous for crystal-clear lagoons, limestone cliffs, and island hopping adventures.

#2 Boracay

Home of the iconic White Beach and vibrant nightlife.

#3 Chocolate Hills

One of the most unique geological formations in the world.

#4 Siargao, Surigao del Norte

The surfing capital of the Philippines, featuring the famous Cloud 9 surf break.

#5 Mayon Volcano, Albay

World-renowned for its perfectly symmetrical cone shape.

#6 Intramuros, Manila

The historic walled city showcasing preserved Spanish colonial architecture.

#7 Coron, Palawan

A top global destination for wreck diving and the stunning Kayangan Lake.

#8 Kawasan Falls, Cebu

Vivid turquoise waterfalls famous for thrilling canyoneering adventures.

#9 Puerto Princesa Subterranean River

A breathtaking UNESCO World Heritage underground river system.

#10 Banaue Rice Terraces, Ifugao

Ancient, emerald-green agricultural terraces carved into the Cordillera mountains.
